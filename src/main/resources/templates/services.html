Our Services

At ContactDiary, we aim to simplify how you manage your contacts securely in the cloud. Whether you're an individual or a team, our services are designed to make your life easier.

🔐 Secure Registration & Login

Create a secure account with email verification and login via Google or GitHub.

📇 Contact Management

Add, edit, search, and delete contact details including name, phone, email, and notes.

⭐ Favorites & Tags

Mark important contacts as favorites for quick access and efficient categorization.

📤 Export to Excel

Instantly export your filtered search results to an Excel file with a single click.

👤 User Profile Settings

Manage your personal profile, update your details, and control your account securely.

☁️ Cloud Sync & Access

Access your contacts from anywhere, on any device, safely stored in the cloud.

🛠️ Technology Stack

Java · Spring Boot · Thymeleaf · MySQL · Tailwind CSS · VS Code · Ubuntu
